DNA By Dennis Kelly

DNA is a craft of all elements of Theatre, from the rich emotion manifested through Lighting and Sound to the schism
created through the Dialogue this piece is has never failed to impress.

It was Originally performed in 2008 at the Dorfman (formally known as 'Cottesloe Theatre'), the staging form used in the performance
was End On however the Dorfman could be configured to an In-The-Round or Thrust form.
