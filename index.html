DNA By Dennis Kelly

DNA is a grandeur craft of all elements of Theatre, from the rich emotion manifested through Lighting and Sound to the folly
created through the Dialogue this piece is brilliant piece of Drama.

Originally performed in 2008 at the Dorfman (formally known as 'Cottesloe Theatre'), the staging form used in the performance
was End On however the Dorfman could be configured to an In-The-Round or Thrust form.
